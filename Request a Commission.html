Commission Terms & Conditions

General Rules

By requesting a commission, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

  • I have the right to refuse any commission request that I feel uncomfortable with or is against my artistic values.
  • Payment is required upfront for all commissions before I begin any work. No refunds once the work has started, except in certain cases where I cannot fulfill the request.
  • Requests will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Estimated time for completion will depend on the complexity of the work and the current workload.

Pricing

The pricing for my commissions varies based on complexity, type, and additional elements. Below is a general guide:

  • Sketches: $10
  • Line Art: $20
  • Full Color (Basic Background): $50
  • Full Color (Detailed Background): $80+
  • Additional Characters: $20 each
  • Extra details or complex requests may require additional fees, which will be discussed beforehand.

Terms of Service

  • All commissioned artwork is for personal use only unless otherwise agreed upon. Commercial use will incur extra fees.
  • Credits must be given if the artwork is posted online (please tag me or mention my username). I retain the right to use the artwork in my portfolio or for promotional purposes.
  • I reserve the right to cancel or refuse a commission if these terms are not followed or if I feel uncomfortable with the request at any point.

Payment Methods

I currently accept payments via PayPal, Venmo, or other secure payment methods. Once your commission is accepted, I will provide you with the payment details.

How to Order

To place a commission, follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the commission request form.
  2. Provide all necessary details for your commission (reference images, descriptions, etc.).
  3. Once I confirm the details, proceed with the payment.
  4. Wait for me to complete your commission, which will be delivered as per the agreed timeline.
